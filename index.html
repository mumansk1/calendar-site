Meet the Founder

At Funny As F*** Therapy, we believe that laughter and honesty can coexist with deep, meaningful work.

Our founder blends sharp clinical skills with irreverent humor to help you feel seen, challenged, and safe enough to be your real self.

Sessions are designed for people who are tired of sugar-coating everything and are ready to talk about the hard stuff with a therapist who can handle it — and maybe even crack a joke when you need it most.
